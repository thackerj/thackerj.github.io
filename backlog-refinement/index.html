Acceptance Criteria

How we know when a PBI meets its requirements

More BDD

Given [context] (and [more context] ...)

When [event]

Then [outcome] (and [another outcome] ...)

Given that there is a new message that I have not seen

When I sign in to EEE

Then I will see a notice that there is a message for me

Given that I have a new message notice

When I click on it

Then I am taken to the message

And the notice goes away
